Question 5

What are the three Scrum artifacts?

[X]Product backlog, sprint backlog, done increment

Correct. The artifacts are the product backlog (stories you have not done yet), the sprint backlog (stories you will do during the sprint), and the Done increment (stories completed by the end of the sprint).
